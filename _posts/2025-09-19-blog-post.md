First, I want to explore 'what'. What is agent? What is RL? what is Agentic RL? What is their relationship?

To put it simple, an agent is an entity which can use tools and take actions to achieve its goals in an environment. A more formal definition can be seen in [<a href="#ref-3">3</a>]. RL is the methodology to train agents. Agentic RL focuses on how RL empowers LLM-based agents in dynamic environments[<a href="#ref-2">2</a>]. As shown in Figure <a href="#img4">4</a>, I draw a diagram to describe their relationships in an intuitive way.
